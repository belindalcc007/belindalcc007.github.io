About Me

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. candidate in The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology(HKUST), supervised by Bertram E. Shi. I received the B.S.(2015) in Information Engineering from Zhejiang University(ZJU), graduated with honor of Zhejiang Province. I entered ECE Ph.D. program (2015) in HKUST with the Hong Kong PhD Fellowship (HKPF) Award.
